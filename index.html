About

I am an assistant professor at Kyung Hee University specializing in computational sociolinguistics, language variation and change, sociophonetics, and corpus-based approaches to Korean linguistics. My research combines quantitative analysis, AI-based methodologies, articulatory phonetics, and large-scale linguistic data to investigate sound change and variation in Korean and English.
